Report app


Report app is an ongoing issue that exists in many ways all over the world, with the reporting of incidents being one of its most important, albeit trickiest aspects. The Report app makes it possible for users to report incidents or harassments experienced by themselves and/or witnessed in their work environment. Besides, users are also able to read tips or information on the topic of workplace harassment and write entries in their own personal logbook.

Tutorial

The user can also learn how to use the app as a tool to navigate incidents in the workplace.

Get Started

To validate the user’s account beforehand, the user must receive their login credentials via email: their work email address along with an unique access code, provided by the user’s employer.

Logbook

On the logbook, the user can create new entries that can serve as evidence and later on compiled and sent with a report.
