EFDN


EFDN (European Football for Development Network) is an organization that promotes the unifying power of football and uses this power as an instrument for social development. The Healthy Football League App is an initiative to promote an active and healthy lifestyle. The app is supported by a large part of the European top clubs and the European Union.

Sign up

Within the app you can select your favorite club and indicate which group you want to join.

Share your performance

You can share your performance on social media channels. The Healthy League Football League App also has a chat function, so you can talk to other users from your selected club.

Ranking

You can keep track of how many kilometers you have covered and collect points for your favorite club. The ranking is classified at national and international level and it is also visible what your ranking is within the club you selected.

The Healthy League Football App also keeps track of your progress, so you can see how many calories you have burned, and how many kilometers you have covered.
